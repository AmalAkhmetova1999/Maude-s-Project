The hotel has 65 fantastic rooms designed to cater for business guests as well as for leisure travellers. Apart from beeing the owner, Maude Ärnström is also the interior designer of the hotel. You will recognize the style from her tho previous hotels, but at the same time it is completely different, but rest assured, the warm atmosphere and homely feeling is certainly still there. With the spacious lobby, the dark grey tones and the element of fire, you get the feeling that you have been transfered to the trendy quarters of New York.

In the lobby you are always welcome to enjoy free tea and coffee with pastries. And in the Lobby Bar you can enjoy a glass of wine or a beer after a long day at work or a day filled with shopping and sightseeing. You can reach Kista Fairs Stockholm with Metro trains and Stockholm International Fairs in Älvsjö with commuter trains.

Airport buses to/from Arlanda stops at Sundbyberg square 7-10 minutes walk Airport buses between Bromma Airport & Arenastaden stop at Solna Hallen (weekdays morning & afternoon) close to the hotel.

If you are travelling by car there are parking, at extra cost, close to the hotel.
