Hotel next to Sundbyberg center, with Metro and commuter trains (only 3 min. walk) to Stockholm city, Solna center and Arenastaden. There are only two stops with the comuting trains to Stockholm City Center. Two stops with the trams (Tvärbanan) to Solna Station and Arenastaden with the national arena Friends Arena and Westfield Mall of Scandinavia. Travelling by underground or tram to Ericsson Globe/Tele2 Arena is a 30-40 minutes journey.

Airport buses to/from Arlanda Airport stop at Sundbyberg square (5 min. walk) and Airport buses between Bromma Airport and Arenastaden stop at Solna Hallen, weekdays morning and afternoon, about 5-10 minutes walk.

Maude´s Hotel Solna Business Park is situated in the area of Solna Business Park which is located on the border between the municipalities of Solna and Sundbyberg. The hotel's design is a blend of Asian and Scandinavian style, which together create a modern, cozy and personal atmosphere. In our lobby lounge we serve a delicious breakfast buffet and you can sit in our glass-enclosed porch and eat your breakfast, drink your coffee or enjoy a glass of wine or beer in the evening. Enjoy our welcoming, casually elegant rooms with high-quality beds whilst relaxing after a hard day work. Internet is included in the room rate and we invite all our guests for coffee or tea with pastries, served in the lobby lounge. Our hotelguests have free access to the gym at the sisterhotel, Hotel By Maude, only 3 minutes walk from the hotel.

It's only 10 minutes by car to Solvalla horse racing tracks. You can reach Kista Fairs ground with commuting- or underground trains. Close to the hotel there are buses to Karolinska University Hospital and Karolinska Institute. Direct commuter trains to Stockholm International Fairs in Älvsjö.

If you are travelling by car there are parking and garage close to the hotel.
