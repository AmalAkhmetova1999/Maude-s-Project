Are you planning to visit Stockholm?

Museums & entertainment park

Don’t miss out visiting Djurgården with ABBA The museum, the Skansen open-air museum and the amazing Vasa museum featuring the world-famous warship Vasa. If you are traveling with children then you will love the Gröna Lund entertainment park and Astrid Lindgren’s Junibacken with its storybook world, restaurant and daily children theaters. The Thielska Gallery and Prince Eugen’s Waldermarsudde are two outstanding art museums, famous for their beautiful collections of Scandinavian paintings and sculptures.

Sightseeing by boat

Stockholm city is spread across 14 islands and you can get to just about all of Stockholm’s many popular tourist attractions on foot, or why not take a boat sightseeing that will give you a different prospect of Scandinavia’s largest and probably most unique city.

The Old town

The oldest part of Stockholm is Gamla Stan - the Old Town, here you can take a nice walk on small picturesque streets with gift and souvenir shops, cozy cafés and romantic restaurants, while visiting historical sites, such as The Royal Palace and the Stockholm Cathedral. We can help with tips on attractions and events, how to book tickets, maps and driving directions, recommend restaurants. Service, helpfulness and thoughtfulness are the guiding principles for those of us working at the hotel to give you that little extra.
